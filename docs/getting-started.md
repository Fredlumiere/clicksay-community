# Getting Started with ClickSay

**Click. Speak. Fix.**

Get up and running with ClickSay in under two minutes.

---

## 1. Install the Extension

Install ClickSay from the [Chrome Web Store](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/clicksay-ai-feedback-copi/cpnmelphcmdcmejmjfnodmpdtifccfhj).

Click **Add to Chrome** and confirm the installation.

## 2. Pin ClickSay to Your Toolbar

After installing, ClickSay may be hidden behind the puzzle piece icon in your toolbar.

1. Click the **puzzle piece** icon (Extensions) in the top-right of Chrome
2. Find **ClickSay** in the list
3. Click the **pin** icon next to it

The ClickSay icon will now always be visible in your toolbar.

## 3. Set Your Keyboard Shortcut

The default shortcut to activate ClickSay is:

- **Mac:** `Cmd + Shift + K`
- **Windows / Linux:** `Ctrl + Shift + K`

To customize the shortcut:

1. Go to `chrome://extensions/shortcuts` in your browser
2. Find **ClickSay** in the list
3. Click the pencil icon and press your preferred key combination
4. Close the tab

## 4. Open the Side Panel

Click the **ClickSay icon** in your toolbar to open the side panel. This is your home base - you'll see your capture history, voice controls, and output settings here.

## 5. Configure Options (Optional)

Right-click the ClickSay icon in your toolbar and select **Options** to access settings:

- **Project Root** - Set your local project root path so ClickSay can map URLs to file paths in your output
- **URL-to-File Mapping** - Choose a preset that matches your project framework:
  - Static Site
  - Next.js
  - SvelteKit
  - React CRA
  - Or create custom mapping rules
- **Pro License Key** - If you have a Pro subscription, enter your license key here to unlock all features

## 6. Your First Capture

1. Navigate to any web page
2. Press `Cmd + Shift + K` (Mac) or `Ctrl + Shift + K` (Win/Linux)
3. Hover over any element - you'll see an **orange outline** as you move your cursor
4. **Click** the element you want to give feedback on
5. Your microphone activates automatically - **speak** your feedback
6. Press **Enter** or click the mic icon to stop recording
7. Press **Enter** or click copy to copy the structured prompt to your clipboard
8. **Paste** into your favorite AI tool

That's it - you're ready to go!
