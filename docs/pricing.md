# ClickSay Pricing

**Click. Speak. Fix.**

ClickSay offers a generous free plan and a Pro plan for power users.

---

## Free Plan - $0

Everything you need to get started, no credit card required.

- **Unlimited** element captures
- **20** voice feedback captures per month
- **5** screenshots per month
- **Simple** and **Standard** output presets
- **Basic** output pills (URL, selector, text, HTML, styles, context, file path)
- **7-day** history retention
- Context menu capture
- Copy to clipboard

---

## Pro Monthly - $9/month

Unlock the full power of ClickSay.

- **Unlimited** element captures
- **Unlimited** voice feedback
- **Unlimited** screenshots
- **All** output presets (Simple, Standard, Detailed, Full)
- **All** output pills (including screenshot, component, a11y)
- **Sweep mode** - capture multiple elements at once
- **Framework detection** - identifies component names and file paths
- **Accessibility audit** - a11y insights in your output
- **Unlimited** history retention

---

## Pro Yearly - $49/year

Same as Pro Monthly, but **55% savings** compared to paying monthly.

That's just over $4/month for the full Pro experience.

---

## How to Upgrade

1. Visit [clicksay.net/#pricing](https://clicksay.net/#pricing)
2. Choose your plan (Monthly or Yearly)
3. Complete checkout via Stripe

## Activate Your License

After purchasing Pro, you'll receive a license key via email.

1. Right-click the ClickSay icon in your toolbar
2. Select **Options**
3. Paste your license key into the **Pro License Key** field
4. Click **Validate**

Your Pro features will activate immediately.
